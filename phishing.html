Real-Life Phishing Examples

Twitter Bitcoin Scam (2020)

Impact: $118,000 Stolen

What Happened:

Hackers gained access to Twitter's internal systems through employee targeting.

Attack Method:

  • Phone spear-phishing
  • Social engineering
  • Employee credential theft

Google Docs Phishing (2017)

Impact: 1 Million Gmail Users

What Happened:

Widespread phishing campaign using fake Google Docs sharing invitations.

Attack Method:

  • OAuth abuse
  • App impersonation
  • Email spoofing
